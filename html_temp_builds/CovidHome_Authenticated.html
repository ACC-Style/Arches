Welcome to the Summer COVID-19 Education Series

The COVID-19 pandemic has and will continue to take its toll on clinicians and the health care system. Each day brings new discoveries — provoking more unanswered questions and posing unprecedented challenges.

To support the health care community through this time, ACC has created a FREE series of targeted and easy-to-consume education to address current and evolving knowledge and practice gaps to help you manage your patients with COVID-19 and heart disease both now and into the future.
