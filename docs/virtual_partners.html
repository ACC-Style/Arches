Industry Partners

ACC would like to express our gratitude to the sponsors who have continued to support the ACC.20/WCC Virtual Conference. As all organizations navigate these challenging times, ACC is deeply appreciative that we have been able to move forward with the continued involvement of some of our closest industry partners. The ACC Scientific Sessions are and always will be a venue that offers the best in practice-changing science and education including new product innovations, technologies and therapies in the field of cardiology. Thank you once again to the industry partners who have continued to support the ACC.20/WCC Virtual Conference.
