Practical Solutions for LDL-C Reduction:An Education Curriculum
These educational resources are crafted to help clinicians enrolled in the TRANSFORM LDL-C Risk Initiative to understand nonstatin therapy options and equip clinical teams to overcome barriers to deliver them to patients.
