1.2 Toggle Icons

Many momment there are needs for toggles of icon when the parent is in the state is-active

.icon-toggle_plus-minus - Toggle between plus and minus square .icon-toggle_chevron-double-up-down - Toggle the double chevron to point up when active .icon-toggle_caret-circle-right-down - Toggle the caret to point down when active. .icon-toggle_arrow-circle - Toggle the arrow to have a circle when active. .icon-toggle_bars-times - Toggle between bars and times
